About GS GATI

GS GATI (School Code: 210713), located in the heart of Nyanza, Rwanda, is a distinguished secondary school committed to academic excellence and holistic student development. As part of Rwanda’s Groupe Scolaire network, we offer a robust curriculum aligned with national education standards, preparing students for advanced studies and professional success. Our dedicated faculty fosters critical thinking, creativity, and leadership through innovative teaching methods. Situated in the historic town of Nyanza, GS GATI benefits from a vibrant community and rich cultural heritage, enhancing our students’ learning experience. Equipped with modern classrooms, science laboratories, and sports facilities, we provide an environment where students thrive academically and socially. Our mission is to nurture responsible citizens who contribute meaningfully to Rwanda’s development and global society.
