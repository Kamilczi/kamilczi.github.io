About G.S KARAMA

G.S KARAMA, located in Nyanza, 210803, Rwanda, is a distinguished educational institution committed to providing high-quality education and nurturing holistic student development. Our school fosters a supportive and inclusive environment, encouraging students to excel academically, socially, and morally. With a dedicated team of educators, G.S KARAMA offers a robust curriculum designed to prepare students for future success. Our modern facilities, including well-equipped science laboratories, a comprehensive library, and expansive sports fields, support both academic excellence and extracurricular growth. We emphasize values of discipline, integrity, and community involvement, aiming to shape students into responsible and compassionate global citizens.
