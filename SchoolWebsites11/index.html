About ES MUZENGA

Located in the vibrant district of Gisagara, Rwanda (220405), ES MUZENGA is a premier educational institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence and holistic development. Our inclusive and supportive environment nurtures intellectual curiosity, social responsibility, and emotional growth. With a committed faculty and a robust curriculum, we prepare students to excel in a dynamic world. Our modern facilities, including well-equipped science labs, a comprehensive library, and spacious sports fields, enhance both academic and extracurricular experiences. At ES MUZENGA, we prioritize discipline, integrity, and community involvement, empowering students to become proactive global citizens.
