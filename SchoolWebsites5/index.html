About GS KATARARA

GS KATARARA, located in Nyanza District, Southern Province, Rwanda (school code: 210804), is a distinguished Groupe Scolaire committed to providing quality education to both primary and secondary students. Situated in the heart of Nyanza, our school fosters a nurturing and inclusive environment that promotes academic excellence, critical thinking, and personal development. Our dedicated faculty delivers a robust curriculum aligned with Rwanda's national education standards, preparing students for future academic and professional success. Equipped with modern facilities, including science laboratories, a library, and sports fields, GS KATARARA supports a holistic educational experience. We emphasize values such as discipline, respect, and community involvement, aiming to shape our students into responsible and engaged citizens of Rwanda and beyond.
