About G.S CYARWA

G.S CYARWA, located in Nyanza, Rwanda (School Code: 211001), is a distinguished educational institution committed to providing high-quality education and fostering holistic development. We offer a vibrant and inclusive learning environment that nurtures students' academic, social, and emotional growth. Our dedicated faculty delivers a comprehensive curriculum designed to prepare students for future challenges. With a focus on discipline, integrity, and community involvement, G.S CYARWA boasts modern facilities, including advanced science laboratories, a well-equipped library, and spacious sports fields, to support both academic and extracurricular activities. Our mission is to empower students to realize their full potential and become responsible global citizens.
