About GS RUGARAMA Nyanza

GS RUGARAMA Nyanza, located in Nyanza District, Rwanda (school code 210807), is a distinguished educational institution committed to providing quality education and nurturing holistic development. Our school fosters a supportive and inclusive environment, encouraging students to excel academically, socially, and morally. With a dedicated team of educators, GS RUGARAMA Nyanza offers a robust curriculum designed to prepare students for future success. Our facilities include modern classrooms, a well-equipped library, and sports areas to support both academic and extracurricular activities. Our mission is to empower students to become innovative, responsible, and compassionate leaders in their communities.
